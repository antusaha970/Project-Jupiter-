Why to code?

  • Code is the foundation of nearly every aspect of our lives, from the apps we use on our phones to the systems that keep our world running smoothly.
  • As a programmer, you'll have the skills to create and build anything you can imagine. You'll also have the chance to solve complex problems and make a real impact on the world.
  • With the right skills, you'll be able to work in a wide variety of industries and roles, from software development to data analysis to machine learning.
  • Mastering a new skill is always rewarding, and the sense of pride and satisfaction you'll feel after successfully completing a project will be worth all the hard work.
  • Learning to code can seem intimidating, but it's important to remember that everyone starts somewhere. Encourage them to start small and take things one step at a time.
